We collect profiles of the authors of each model, simulation, and visualization. This includes their name, institution, website, email, ORCID, and a brief biography. In addition, we collect standard information about user behavior such as the IP addresses, operating systems, and browsers used to access our website and the dates and times that users visit our website. We use the author profiles to publicly credit authors for their models, simulations, and visualizations. We use the user behavior data to help improve the functionality of {{ config.appName }} for the scientific community. This includes helping us better understand the needs of users. After logging in, authors can edit their profile using our online form. We use Google Analytics to collect data about user behavior. Google Analytics uses cookies. The author profiles are stored in our MongoDB database hosted by MongoDB Atlas. We use Google Analytics to store the user behavior data. Personal data will not be transferred to international organizations. This personal data will be stored as long as {{ config.appName }} is live. The author profiles are publicly accessible. The user behavior data is only accessible to the leader developers. We may also make information about the total volume of usage of particular simulation studies and simulation tools available to the public and third party organizations who supply the studies and tools without details of any individual's use. You have the right to view, edit, or delete your author profile at any time. You also have the right to request at reasonable intervals, and without excessive delay or expense, information about the user behavior data processed about you.
